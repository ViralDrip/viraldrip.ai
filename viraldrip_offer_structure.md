## What's Included

### Phase 1: Setup (One-Time)

**1. Speed-to-Lead System**
- Instant personalized follow-up within minutes of opt-in
- Email and/or SMS response automation
- AI-generated personalized messages based on lead data
- Integration with existing landing page or opt-in form

**2. Lead Capture & Segmentation**
- Optimized opt-in flow with strategic questions
- Automatic tagging based on responses (segment by problem/interest)
- UTM and source tracking where applicable
- Connection to CRM/database (Airtable or client's existing tool)

**3. AI-Powered Email Nurture Sequence**
- Behavior-based sequences (not static drip campaigns)
- Branching logic based on opens, clicks, and engagement
- AI-personalized copy that matches client's voice
- Multiple paths based on lead score and actions

**4. Lead Scoring System**
- Automatic scoring based on engagement behavior
- Score updates on opens, clicks, replies, and conversions
- High-score alerts for hot leads ready to convert
- Visual dashboard in Airtable or CRM

**5. Re-Engagement Automation**
- Automated outreach to cold leads (no activity 7+ days)
- AI-personalized re-engagement messages
- Scheduled checks running daily
- Win-back sequences before leads go fully cold

**6. Reporting Dashboard**
- Overview of lead flow and conversion metrics
- Visibility into what's working and what's not
- Lead status tracking (new, engaged, hot, converted, cold)
- Updated automatically

---

### Phase 2: Retainer (Ongoing Monthly)

**Monitoring & Maintenance**
- System health checks
- Bug fixes and error handling
- Platform updates and compatibility maintenance

**Optimization**
- A/B testing subject lines and messages
- Sequence performance analysis
- Conversion rate optimization recommendations

**Support & Consultation**
- Priority support for questions and requests
- Monthly check-in call (optional)
- AI and automation consultation for new ideas
- Small updates and tweaks as needed

## Delivery Timeline

### Week 1: Onboarding & Strategy
- Kickoff call (60 min)
- Access collection and tool audit
- Strategy session: funnel mapping, messaging direction
- Brand voice review (existing emails, content, tone)

### Week 2–3: Build & Test
- System build in Make.com
- AI prompt development and personalization setup
- CRM/database configuration
- Email sequence creation
- Integration and connection of all platforms
- Internal testing and QA

### Week 4: Launch & Optimize
- Final review call with client
- Live launch
- Monitoring for first 7 days
- Initial optimizations based on real data
- Handoff and retainer kickoff

**Total delivery time: 3–4 weeks**

## What the Client Needs to Provide

### Access & Accounts
- Login or admin access to email platform (or willingness to set one up)
- Access to landing page builder or website
- Access to existing CRM if applicable
- Skool admin access (for understanding their setup)

### Brand Assets
- Examples of past emails or content (for voice matching)
- Brand guidelines if they exist (colors, tone, logo)
- Headshot/photo for personalized elements if needed

### Content & Offers
- Existing lead magnet or willingness to create one
- Clear description of their paid Skool offer
- Any existing funnel or sequence to review

### Time Commitment
- Kickoff call: 60 minutes
- Feedback/review call: 30–60 minutes
- Async communication for questions: ~1–2 hours total
- Final review call: 30 minutes

**Total client time investment: ~3–4 hours across 4 weeks**

## Scope Boundaries

### Included
- Everything listed above in Setup and Retainer sections
- Integrations with standard tools (Make.com, Airtable, standard email platforms, Twilio)
- AI personalization using OpenAI API
- Documentation for how the system works

### Not Included
- Paid ad management or ad creative
- Landing page design or copywriting (unless added)
- Course or community content creation
- Tools/software subscription costs (client pays their own subscriptions)
- Major rebuilds or new systems (scoped separately)
